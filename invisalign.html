Invisalign

Platinum Elite Provider in Chiswick.

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made, virtually invisible aligners to gradually straighten your teeth — without the brackets or wires of traditional braces. As a Platinum Elite Invisalign provider, Chiswick Park Dental has the expertise to deliver outstanding results for patients of all ages.

Treatment typically takes 6 to 18 months depending on complexity. You'll wear each set of aligners for 1 to 2 weeks, removing them only to eat, drink, brush, and floss. Most patients notice visible improvement within the first few weeks.

Frequently asked questions

Each set of aligners is 3D-printed to apply gentle, controlled force to specific teeth. You'll progress through a series of aligners — each one moving your teeth slightly closer to their final position. Your dentist will monitor progress every 6 to 8 weeks.
You may feel mild pressure when switching to a new set of aligners — this is the treatment working. Most patients find it far more comfortable than traditional braces. Any discomfort typically passes within a day or two.
Invisalign Lite (minor cases) typically takes 6 to 9 months. Comprehensive cases take 12 to 18 months. Your dentist will give you an accurate timeline at your free consultation after scanning your teeth.
Invisalign Lite starts from £2,500 and Comprehensive from £4,000. We offer 0% interest-free finance to spread the cost. Your exact price is confirmed at your free consultation.
