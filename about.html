About Us

Over 30 years of trusted dental care in Chiswick.

Your local dental practice

Led by specialist oral surgeon Dr Fabrizio Rapisarda, Chiswick Park Dental & Facial has been at the heart of the Chiswick community for over 30 years. Our team provides comprehensive dental and facial aesthetic treatments in a comfortable, modern environment.

We speak English, Italian, French, Polish, Persian, and Arabic — so everyone feels at home.
